Vision
To be an Institute of excellence with total commitment to quality education in engineering, technology, management and other related fields, with a holistic concern for better life and employability and to prepare the entrepreneurs to be proud citizens who can contribute to the prosperity of the state.

Mission
The Institute pursues the aim to become an important link in the chain of leading centers of technical and creative education. The central concern here is to nurture creative skills and advanced technologies to contribute to creation, application and extension of knowledge for the welfare of society.
