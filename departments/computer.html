Computer Engineering (No: of Seats:- 40)

This branch deals with design and construction of computers, development of computer programs (software) and computer based information and control systems. In its most general form it is concerned with the understanding of information compilation, transfer and transformation. Institute has well equipped computer labs with all new up to date software and nearly 80 p-4 computers. All the systems are interlinked with structured cabling and with Internet facilities provided through dedicated lease line based (speed up to 4 Mbps). Labs are fully air conditioned.

Salient Features of the Course
1. Programme Title Diploma course in Computer Engineering.
2. Entry Qualification Pass in Metric Examination from State Board of School Education, Haryana.
3. Course Duration 3 years
4. Programme Structure Semester system

Students in the department have access to a wide variety of computer equipment, including an extensive network and Intel-based machines. In addition, there are specialized instructional and research labs for database systems, parallel and distributed computing, security, software engineering, wireless networks, computer graphics, vision and image processing, and web search technology, which are used in advanced courses and research projects
