CIVIL DEPARTMENT

Civil Engineering is one of the oldest branches of Engineering. Civil Engineers are concerned with all types of structures including dams, bridges, pipelines, roads, towers and buildings, transport systems, the design and management of our gas and water supply, sewerage systems, harbors, airports and railways. The department is fully reformative and continuously reshaping its laboratory features, faculty and other requirements as per the demand of time and region. The department also takes care of the level of training required for placement of its student not only with in the country but also across its borders and meet out the new challenges faced by our human resources to exploit the opportunities available over there.
