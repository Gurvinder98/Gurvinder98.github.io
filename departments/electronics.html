Electronics Department

Electronics Engineering (No: of Seats:- 40)

The ever increasing demand for faster and novel means of audio and video communication systems and the automation in industry etc. have made the Electronics Engineers and indispensable member of the modern society. Under the worsening energy crisis, the role of an electronic engineer comprises the design and development of more efficient electronic machinery, power systems and control equipment and systems. with ever increasing tempo of events throughout the world, it has become essential to have more efficient communication network with the latest electronic devices and circuits. The department has well-equipped laboratories such as principle of communication engineering lab., Communication system lab., Advanced communication system lab., Communication Engineering lab., TV lab., Digital Electronics lab., Electronic and Devices laboratories, Introduction to Microprocessor lab., Industrial Engg. lab., Network Filters & Transmission lab., Computer lab. & Electrical Laboratory. This department offers three years diploma in Electronics and Comm. Engg. This department has following fully equipped labs with state of the art equipments: -

  • Electronics Devices and Circuits
  • Power Electronics
  • Digital Electronics
  • Microprocessors
  • Consumer Electronics
  • Communication Lab
  • Electronics Workshop
  • Robotics Lab
  • Documentation Centre
